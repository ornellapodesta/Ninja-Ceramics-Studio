Our Story

ABOUT
US

We are an art brand dedicated to exploring the relationship between objects, space, and context. Each piece challenges the conventional boundaries of functional art.

Handcraft as a philosophy, not just a method.

Ninja Ceramics was born in Barcelona from a deep belief that the objects we live with should hold meaning. We work with clay, metal, and found materials to create pieces that carry the memory of the hands that made them.

Our studio is a space of ongoing inquiry — where technique is a starting point, not a destination. Traditional methods are learned, then subverted. Each piece emerges from a conversation between material and intention.

We run workshops, regular classes, and an open studio because we believe the act of making is something worth sharing. Come with curiosity, leave with clay on your hands.
